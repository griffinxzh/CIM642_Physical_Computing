# Summary

This is a Dodecahedron Terrarium that I designed as a centerpiece. It is 8 inches tall, and 10 inches wide, so it probably won't fit on most peoples printers. I've included a single side and a 4 side plate that can be used on printers with beds greater than 300 mm. I'd recommend printing either of the plates and gluing the sides together.
